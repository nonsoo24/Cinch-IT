    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What does claim application mean?
    Claim application is when you notify your insurer about a damage that happened to your device, and you would want to fix it based on the insurance purchased.
    How can i make a claim application?
    You can make a claim application by logging into your account and navigate to the claim history, then click on the add new claim.
    What do I need to make a claim application?
    You will need to upload an image of the damaged phone while filling the application form
    What next after the application?
    You will receive an email that details the service centre that will fix your device. You can also regularly check the claim history page to know the status of your application.
